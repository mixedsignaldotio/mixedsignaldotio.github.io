Prof. Lisa Wang

RF & Wireless Communication

Prof. Lisa Wang is a renowned expert in RF and wireless communication systems, specializing in 5G mmWave transceivers and wireless infrastructure design. Her research focuses on overcoming path loss and phase noise challenges in next-generation communication systems.

She holds a Ph.D. from UC Berkeley and has collaborated with leading telecommunications companies to develop innovative solutions for high-frequency wireless systems.
