Dr. Michael Zhang

Power Electronics Engineer

Dr. Michael Zhang is a leading researcher in gallium nitride (GaN) power converters and high-efficiency DC-DC conversion systems. He has been instrumental in developing next-generation power management solutions for automotive and industrial applications.

He earned his Ph.D. from MIT and has worked with major semiconductor companies to bring cutting-edge power conversion technology from research to production. His expertise includes wide-bandgap semiconductors, power system optimization, and thermal management.
